Uxn Zine

Rostiger made an awesome comic explaining how Uxn works, so I compiled the drawings into a pdf designed to be printed and made into a zine. It turned out pretty well, and the template is simple enough to be reused. You can download the pdf or epub directly, or download a tar of all of files and resources to compile it yourself. Assembly is mostly as describer in this article, but once folded according to the directions, staple the spine together and cut the tops and bottoms of the few pages still connected. It's a little jank, but it works!

It's made with two latex files, the first (source.tex) of which adds each photo to its own page in a pdf, and the second (zine.tex) which references the first to lay out the pages correctly.
